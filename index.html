What is GSAP?

2018 Samsung global startup acceleration program is a part of Samsung employee volunteer program. Since 2010, about 1,500 employees have joined this program in 43 countries. Specially this year, Samsung is focused on the local startup to support local enterpreneurs to build their own business and growth for sustainable impact in society. This program is being held globally in Indonesia, Malaysia, India, South Africa in this year from July to September and About 80 selected startups are going to join in this program. here we have 20 startups are joining in India from 6 to 10, August at IIITB in Bangalore.

This programs is going to be running for a week and at the end of the program, there will an pitching event. Any participants which has business potential will get the extended support from the local incubation center for the next 6 months. Among winners in 4 countries, 5 startup will be invited to Samsung head quater in Korea.
